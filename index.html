Tetris Game

To play, simply click "start", and use your keyboard or the buttons provided to move the tetrinomoes!

The goal is to line them up - as soon as a row is covered, they'll disappear. Don't let them get to the top!

The left arrow moves the piece left, right arrow moves it right, down pushes it down faster, and up rotates the piece.

As you eliminate rows, your score increases! As your score increases, you level up. As you level up, the falling speed gets faster!

Good luck!
